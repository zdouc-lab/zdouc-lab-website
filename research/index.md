# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-microscope" %}Research

Our research covers the whole microbial natural product discovery process. 
It spans tool development for metabolomics and genomics data mining, data analysis,
wet-lab investigation, compound isolation, and biological characterization.
We also provide our expertise via reviews and perspective/opinion articles. 

The overarching approach to our research is focused towards the [FAIR principles](https://www.nature.com/articles/sdata201618),
emphasizing transparency, reproducibility, and rigor.
